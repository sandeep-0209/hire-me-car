HireMeCar

HireMeCar is India's largest cloud based digital discovery and services provisioning platform for the taxi rental Industry. Serving over 7000 customers in 100+ cities, the platform facilitates more than 20,000 Kms of safe and seamless travel on a daily basis for travelers and service providers over the length and breadth of the country.

More than 90% of the taxi hires market in India are handled by unorganized and small players. The platform aims at serving the needs of more than 1.5 Million (15 Lac) independent taxi operators and millions of customers by providing them with seamless cloud-based service provisioning and digital interfaces for their travel needs.
